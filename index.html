How it works

  1. 1

    Sign in and create an organization.

    We mint a per-tenant ingest token and assign a tracking id automatically.

  2. 2

    Drop the loader on your page.

    Paste this once in your <head>, replacing VIDALY_ID with your tracking id. Every <video> on the page gets watched-segment tracking automatically. 

    <script id="vidalyy">
(function(w,d,s,l,i){
  w[l]=w[l]||function(){(w[l].q=w[l].q||[]).push(arguments)};
  w[l].id=i; w[l].t=+new Date;
  var f=d.getElementsByTagName(s)[0],
      j=d.createElement(s);
  j.async=true; j.src='https://cdn.vidalyy.example/collector.min.js';
  j.setAttribute('data-vidaly-id',i);
  f.parentNode.insertBefore(j,f);
})(window,document,'script','vidalyy','VIDALY_ID');
</script>
  3. 3

    Watch the dashboard fill in.

    Segment heatmaps, lifecycle events, and per-source retention update in real time as visitors watch.
